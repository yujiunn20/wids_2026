Data structure and an training example

event_id: 11757157

* Receive: Features computed strictly from the first five hours after initial perimeter detection. These represent early spread dynamics and spatial relationships to evacuation zones.
* Predict: Four probabilities for each fire. Each probability represents the likelihood that the fire comes within 5 km of an evacuation zone centroid by the horizon. prob_12h prob_24h prob_48h prob_72h.


Labels: 

- Temporal Coverage (first 5h only)
1. num_perimeters_0_5h: Number of perimeters within first 5 hours, ex. 2
2. dt_first_last_0_5h: Time span between first and last perimeter (hours), ex. 1.1699176616666669
3. low_temporal_resolution_0_5h: Flag, 1 if dt < 0.5h or only 1 perimeter, else 0, ex. 0

- Growth Features
4. area_first_ha: Initial fire area at t0 (hectares), ex. 8.946749378249011
5. area_growth_abs_0_5h: Absolute area growth (hectares), ex. 0.0
6. area_growth_rel_0_5h: Relative area growth (fraction), ex. 0.0
7. area_growth_rate_ha_per_h: Area growth rate (hectares/hour), ex. 0.0

8. log1p_area_first: Log(1 + initial area), ex. 2.2972458021404853
9. log1p_growth: Log(1 + absolute growth),  ex. 0.0
10. log_area_ratio_0_5h: Log ratio of final to initial area, ex. 0.0 
11. relative_growth_0_5h: Relative growth (same as area_growth_rel_0_5h), ex. 0.0 

12. radial_growth_m: Change in effective radius (meters), ex. 0.0
13. radial_growth_rate_m_per_h: Rate of radial growth (meters/hour), ex. 0.0

- Centroid Kinematics
14. centroid_displacement_m: Total displacement of fire centroid (meters), ex. 0.0
15. centroid_speed_m_per_h: Speed of centroid movement (meters/hour), ex. 0.0
16. spread_bearing_deg: Bearing/direction of fire spread (degrees), ex. 0.0
17. spread_bearing_sin: Sine of spread bearing (circular encoding), ex. 0.0
18. spread_bearing_cos: Cosine of spread bearing (circular encoding), ex. 1.0

- Distance to Evacuation Zone Centroids
19. dist_min_ci_0_5h: Minimum distance to nearest evac zone centroid (meters), ex. 2930.925956098736
20. dist_std_ci_0_5h: Standard deviation of distances, ex. 0.0
21. dist_change_ci_0_5h: Change in distance (d5 - d0, negative = closing), ex. 0.0
22. dist_slope_ci_0_5h: Linear slope of distance vs time (meters/hour), ex. -3.88700304121087e-13
23. closing_speed_m_per_h: Speed at which fire is closing distance (m/hour, positive = closing), ex. 0.0
24. closing_speed_abs_m_per_h: Absolute closing speed, ex. 0.0
25. projected_advance_m: Projected advance toward evac zone (d0 - d5), ex. 0.0
26. dist_accel_m_per_h2: Acceleration in distance change (meters/hour^2), ex. 0.0
27. dist_fit_r2_0_5h: R^2 of linear fit to distance vs time, ex. 0.0

- Directionality
28. alignment_cos: Cosine of angle between fire motion and evac direction, ex. -0.5688982113791312
29. alignment_abs: Absolute alignment between fire motion and evac direction (0-1, higher = more aligned), ex. 0.5688982113791312
30. cross_track_component: Sideways drift component, ex. -0.0
31. along_track_speed: Speed component toward/away from evac, ex. -0.0

- Temporal Metadata
32. event_start_hour: Hour of day when fire started (0-23), ex. 4
33. event_start_dayofweek: Day of week (0 = Monday, 6 = Sunday), ex. 4
34. event_start_month: Month when fire started (1-12), ex. 6

*** Target(train only)
time_to_hit_hours: Time from t0+5h until fire comes within 5 km of an evac zone (hours). For censored events (never hit within 72h), this is the last observed time within the window (<= 72). ex. 22.04810800805556
event: Event indicator, 1 if fire hit within 72h, 0 if censored (never hit), ex. 1


Acronyms and Short Terms
- t0: Fire start time (first perimeter observation)
- 5h: Feature window length (first 5 hours after t0)
- evac: Evacuation zone
- ci: Centroid-to-centroid distance
